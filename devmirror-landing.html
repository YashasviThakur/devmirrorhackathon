— The 6-Option Menu

Ask DevMirror anything
about your dev life.

The main menu is six questions you actually care about. Pick one, and DevMirror fetches your data live and gets Gemini to answer — honestly, helpfully, and without judgement.

01 ──────────
Give me my growth report
Full AI synthesis of your week — wins across all 6 platforms, patterns, and personalised nudges.
02 ──────────
How is my DSA progress?
LeetCode + Codeforces combined view — streak status, difficulty breakdown, what to tackle next.
03 ──────────
What should I focus on today?
Gemini reads your calendar, GitHub activity, and DSA progress to recommend your single best move.
04 ──────────
Am I learning more than building?
YouTube watch history vs GitHub commits. Are you consuming or creating? Get an honest balance score.
05 ──────────
Any internship or hackathon emails?
Gmail filtered and summarised for opportunities. Application windows, invites, and deadlines surfaced automatically.
06 ──────────
Exit
When you're done growing for the day.

— Dead Simple Setup

Up and running
in three steps.

01
Clone & Configure

Clone the repo, add your GITHUB_TOKEN and GEMINI_API_KEY to .env. Authenticate Gmail and Calendar via OAuth once — then you're set.

02
Run the Agent

python main.py launches the 6-option interactive menu. Pick a question. DevMirror fetches data from all six sources in parallel and sends it to Gemini.

03
Read Your Report

Gemini 2.5 Flash synthesises everything into a personalised report, formatted beautifully in your terminal by the Rich library. Your coach, in your terminal.
